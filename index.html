How to Use Our Random Tools - Complete Guide

Random Number Selection

Need to pick a number 1-100 quickly? Use our Random Number Generator to select from any range. Whether you need classroom picks, raffle numbers, or lottery selections, simply set your minimum and maximum values and generate. Popular ranges include 1-10 for small groups, 1-100 for percentages, and custom ranges for specific needs.

Dice Rolling for Gaming

Our dice roller multiple feature lets you roll up to 5 dice simultaneously - perfect for board games and damage calculations. For tabletop RPG players, try our specialized dice roller D&D mode with polyhedral dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, d100). The DnD dice online tool includes advantage/disadvantage rolling and automatic critical hit detection for D&D 5e campaigns.

Decision Making Tools

Can't decide what to do? Our pick wheel (also called a decision wheel) helps you choose between multiple options. Create custom wheels with 2-20 sections for any decision. Popular uses include the "what should I do wheel" for daily activities, restaurant choices, movie selections, and group decisions. Perfect for indecisive moments!

Coin Flipping & Quick Choices

The coin flip simulator provides classic heads-or-tails decisions with realistic animation. Great for settling disputes, making binary choices, or when you need a simple yes/no answer. Track your flip history and statistics over time.
