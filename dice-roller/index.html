About this Tabletop Dice Roller

This free online dice roller lets you roll 1–5 dice with smooth animation, lock individual dice, and track basic stats. It's perfect for tabletop nights, classrooms, and quick decisions.

Great for RPGs: use it as a fast dice roller d&d companion during sessions, or for board games where physical dice aren't handy.

How it works

Each roll uses a browser-based pseudorandom process. Rolls are independent; probabilities reset every time you press Space or hit Roll. The UI shows sum, average, and your best roll for the current session.

Tips & use cases
  • Press Space (or tap) to start rolling; press again to end the animation faster.
  • Click a die to lock it, then re-roll the rest—handy for games that allow partial re-rolls.
  • Go full screen for stream overlays or big tables.
  • Need other tools? Try the Random Number Generator, Coin Flip, or Decision Wheel.
FAQ

Is this board game dice roller fair?

It's designed to be unbiased over many rolls. The randomness is pseudorandom and suitable for everyday play and demos.

Does it support D&D?

For D&D games, we recommend our dedicated D&D Dice Roller with d20, d12, d10, d8, d6, d4, advantage/disadvantage, and modifiers. This dice roller is better suited for general board games and simple d6 rolls.

Do you save my rolls?

No. Rolling happens locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for site-wide analytics details.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Once the page is cached (e.g., as a PWA), the roller works without a network connection.
