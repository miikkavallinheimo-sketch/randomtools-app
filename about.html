About RandomTools.app

RandomTools.app is a collection of fast, privacy-friendly randomization tools: a coin flipper to flip a coin, an online dice roller (great as a dice roller D&D helper), a random number generator / selector, and a decision wheel for “wheel of fortune” style picks.

Why we built it

  • Zero friction: No sign-up or paywall. Open the page and go.
  • Runs in your browser: Flips, rolls and numbers are generated locally. Your results aren’t uploaded.
  • Presentation-ready: Full screen modes, clear animations, and keyboard shortcuts (Space to start/stop).
  • Works offline: If the page is cached (PWA), tools keep working without network access.

How it works (randomness)

Our tools use a browser-based pseudorandom process suited for everyday choices, classrooms, streams, and games. Results are independent per run; over many runs, outcomes should look unbiased. (They are not meant for cryptographic security or regulated gambling.)

Who it’s for

  • Teachers & facilitators: pick students or topics fairly.
  • Streamers & event hosts: big, clean visuals in full screen.
  • Gamers & RPG groups: quick d6 rolls and tie-breaks.
  • Teams & families: decide what to do, watch or eat.

Roadmap

  • Optional polyhedral dice sets (d4, d8, d10, d12, d20).
  • Lightweight session stats and export (CSV/JSON).
  • Custom themes and accessibility presets.

Privacy and trust

We don’t store your individual results. Any preferences (like last used settings) may live in your browser storage. See our Privacy Policy for details.
